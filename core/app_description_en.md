ESP POS is a complete business management app built for Myanmar shop owners, money exchange agents, and small retailers. Manage mobile wallet exchange, daily cash, product sales, stock, and profit — all from your phone or tablet.

MONEY EXCHANGE & E-WALLET MANAGEMENT
• Track KBZPay, Wave Money, Wave Merchant, KBZ Mobile Banking, CB Pay, UAB Pay, AYA Pay, Yoma Bank, Mytel Pay, and custom wallets
• Agent cash in / cash out for cash and e-money
• Manage cash balance and wallet accounts in one dashboard
• Transfer and receive with fee tracking
• Receivables and payables for money you are owed or need to pay
• Transaction history with search and date filters
• Profit dashboard with charts and analytics

MINI POS & RETAIL SALES
• Fast checkout with product search and barcode scanning
• Product lists, stock in, and stock history
• Promotions and discount pricing
• Invoices, refunds, and receipt printing
• Merchants, staff members, and role-based access
• Accounts receivable / payable (AR/AP) for customer and supplier balances
• Top-selling products, general costs, and investment tracking
• Sale dashboard with daily, monthly, and yearly reports
• Selling price calculator for margin planning

BLUETOOTH RECEIPT PRINTING
• Connect a Bluetooth thermal printer
• Print sales receipts and invoices at checkout

LOCAL NETWORK SYNC
• Connect multiple devices on the same Wi-Fi network
• Use one main device as the server and extra counters as clients
• Sync sales and exchange data without cloud dependency
• PIN-protected local connection for shop staff

BACKUP, SECURITY & SETTINGS
• Backup and restore your data to Google Drive
• Activity logs and deleted history
• Dark and light theme
• English and Myanmar (Burmese) language support
• Stock low-level alerts

WHO IS ESP POS FOR?
• Mobile money and wallet exchange agents
• Grocery stores, mini marts, and retail counters
• Shops that sell products and handle e-wallet payments daily
• Businesses that need simple profit tracking without complicated ERP software

Start with a trial and upgrade to Premium for full access to advanced features. ESP POS keeps your exchange balances, sales records, and business reports organized — offline-first on your device, with optional backup and multi-device sync when you need it.

Note: ESP POS is a business record-keeping tool. It does not process payments directly through wallet providers; you record transactions you perform in your own wallet apps.
