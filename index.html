Everything your shop needs

From mobile-money exchange to retail checkout, ESP POS keeps your whole business organized in one place.

💵

Money Exchange & e-Wallet

Track KBZPay, Wave Money, Wave Merchant, CB Pay, AYA, UAB, Yoma, Mytel Pay and custom wallets. Agent cash in / cash out with fee tracking, receivables, and a profit dashboard.

🛒

POS & Retail Sales

Fast checkout with product search and barcode scanning, stock in and history, promotions and discounts, invoices, refunds, AR/AP, and daily/monthly/yearly sale reports.

🖨️

Bluetooth Receipt Printing

Connect a Bluetooth thermal printer and print sales receipts and invoices instantly at checkout.

🌐

Local Network Sync

Connect multiple phones and tablets on the same Wi-Fi. Run one device as the server and extra counters as clients - no internet or cloud needed, PIN-protected.

🛡️

Backup & Security

Back up and restore to Google Drive, view activity logs and deleted history, get stock low-level alerts, and switch between dark and light themes.

🏷️

One-time price

Start with a free trial and upgrade to Premium for full access. No complicated ERP - just simple, organized profit tracking.
